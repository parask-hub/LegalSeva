Our Services

FAMILY LAW

Family law deals with family matters and domestic relations, including marriage and civil partnerships, the termination of relationships and child law. Legal Seva aims to empower the average person to know their rights and dispel the taboo around getting legal help.

EDUCATION LAW

The Education law is a branch of Civil Law and deals with the laws, regulations, policies, and instructions that govern and regulate Federal and State Education, and also the education provided by the private institutions, from the pre-nursery school level to the higher education in universities.

TRANSPORTATION LAW

Transportation Law is the body of law that governs transportation infrastructure and its use. It regulates the way that people travel using any method of transportation including railways, air travel, vehicular travel and even waterways.

TRADE LAW

International trade laws are those areas of law that deal with certain rules and customs regarding the handling of trade between countries. It is also used for trade between two private sector companies in two countries.

CRIMINAL LAW

Criminal law deals with offences against society. It is a devic3 by which organised society protects the security of individual interests and ensures the survival of the group.

INSURANCE LAW

Insurance is a means of protection from financial loss. It is a form of risk management primarily used to hedge against the risk of a contingent, uncertain loss

MEDICAL LAW

Medical Law: is the body of law governing the rights and obligations of doctors and their patients. Healthcare in India has a universal health system operated by the member states and territories. The law is an obligation imposed by the competent authority, the failure of which would result in punishment in the form of monetary compensation or imprisonment, or both.
